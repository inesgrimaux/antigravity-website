Professional Biography

Ines Grimaux is a contemporary artist and environmentalist whose multidisciplinary practice spans photography, drawing, painting, and sculpture. Based for many years on the Island of Itaparica, Bahia, she has been a dedicated supporter of various NGOs, integrating her artistic vision with environmental activism and territorial development.

Her work explores perception beyond appearance, creating contemplative visual experiences that manifest as a continuous observation of existence. In her practice, photography often serves as a powerful medium for performance documentation, notably the Siren Jones series—a collaboration with performer Elizabeth Doud.
